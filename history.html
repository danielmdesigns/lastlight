The abstract, experimental & limited edition artwork of danielmdesigns. Including album layout, website development & merchandise designs for hardcore punk rock bands, boutiques & tattoo studios.

Daniel Maldonado is a artist and designer based in Austin, Texas. Obsessing over all things art, music, design, and football.

Daniel first got his start in design creating old school punk rock flyers, posters, demo covers and tshirt designs. These were the nights spent at Kinko's photocopying, cutting, and pasting to create one of a kind flyers that could then be handed out around town to promote upcoming gigs.

The band eventually started to tour and gain populatirty, which required more merchandise. Eventually, Daniel and fellow guitar player decided that they could save costs by learning and printing the merch themselves. They quickly started a small DIY print shop. Meanwhile, touring opened plenty of doors to meet other bands and promote the design & print venture. Of course, we eventually went our seperate ways and Daniel ran a print studio for a few years after that until deciding to go back to school.

During that time, Daniel was working as a silkscreen art pre-press designer and art specialist for an online sign company. Working full time and going to art school full time.

Visual Interface and User Experience Design eventually became Daniel's focus for web and mobile products. However, the roots of good ole DIY design and band record covers and merchandise design still fuels inside.

This is the passion and side project of Daniel Maldonado, who still enjoy's keeping things DIY and offering more abstract design services to bands, tattoo artist, and boutiques.
