Thumbscrew "Enlightenment" 2xLP Discography

“Enlightenment” is an expansive 2xLP combining both “All Is Quiet” and “Within Hearts of Redemption” from mathy moshcore masters, Thumbscrew.

Only available as a one-time, limited edition pressing of 300, across 5 amazing variants, from Wax Vessel.

Illustrations were carried out by the always amazing Alexandre Goulet while I provided additional layout, typography, texture and insert(s) design.

The 2xLP discography came housed inside of a 1.5x thick matte gatefold jacket. Double-sided matte insert with black poly lined inner sleeves, gold foil numbering all stuffed into polybags.

DSGNS "Ceremony" Hand Painted Album Design & Layout
