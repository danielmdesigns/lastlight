DSGNS "Ceremony" Hand Painted Album Design & Layout

This album design features over five 9"x12" original hand painted pieces of art. Each individually scanned and edited in Photoshop where handmade textures were also used. Complete with full design, layout and packaging.

“Ceremony” is a six song EP from DSGNS, a hardcore punk rock band based in Austin, TX. Recorded at The Red Room in Seattle, WA with Matt Bayles (Mastodon, Botch), vocals recorded at Suite 2e in Austin, TX with Kieth Hernandez (Drip-Fed, Insvrgence) and mastering by Brad Boatright (Integrity, Nails) at Audiosiege in Portland, OR.

Track listing:

01. Dead Husk
02. Crooked Teeth
03. Zealot
04. Ceremony
05. Hungry Tombs
06. Bolter
