DSGNS "Ceremony" Hand Painted Album Design & Layout

This album design features over five 9"x12" original hand painted pieces of art. Each individually scanned and edited in Photoshop where handmade textures were also used. Complete with full design, layout and packaging.

“Ceremony” is a six song EP from DSGNS, a hardcore punk rock band based in Austin, TX. Recorded at The Red Room in Seattle, WA with Matt Bayles (Mastodon, Botch), vocals recorded at Suite 2e in Austin, TX with Kieth Hernandez (Drip-Fed, Insvrgence) and mastering by Brad Boatright (Integrity, Nails) at Audiosiege in Portland, OR.

Track listing:

01. Dead Husk
02. Crooked Teeth
03. Zealot
04. Ceremony
05. Hungry Tombs
06. Bolter

Blurry Vision "Bloodletting" CD Design & Layout

Album artwork and layout design for Blurry Vision's first debut full length album "Bloodletting". The band also had 100% cotton "Black" T-shirt printed, front & back, in support of "Bloodletting".

Blurry Vision was a three piece hardcore punk rock band, based in Austin, Texas. The band consists of Jeffrey Blum (guitar, vocals) John Nichols (bass, vocals) and Stephen Miller (drums).

Track listing:

02. Nine Years
03. Bad Blood
04. Jacob's Broken Ladder
05. Slumset
06. Prescription Religion
07. Black Hole
08. Saint Morphine
09. Untitled
